I'm a 2nd Year Masters Student in Computer Science @ University of British Columbia.
Member of the Networks, Systems and Security Lab (NSS).
I finished my undergraduate in 2016 @ University of Waterloo Systems Design Engineering.

My current research interests include machine learning, differential privacy, distributed systems and security. I work on problems that intersect these spaces under the helpful supervision of Ivan Beschastnikh. I also currently serve as the president of the Computer Science Graduate Student's Association (CSGSA).
