Thank you for visiting!

I'm a 2nd Year Masters Student in Computer Science @ University of British Columbia and a member of the Networks, Systems and Security (NSS) Lab. I work under the helpful supervision of Ivan Beschastnikh.

Prior to this, I finished my undergraduate in 2016 @ University of Waterloo Systems Design Engineering.

My current research interests include distributed systems (current flavor of the month is blockchain), distributed ML, attacks on privacy in distributed ML, and corresponding defenses in distributed ML. Lately, I am especially interested in federated learning.
