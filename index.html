Summary

Thank you for visiting!

I'm a 2nd Year MSc Computer Science student at the University of British Columbia and a member of the Networks, Systems and Security (NSS) Lab. I work under the helpful supervision of Ivan Beschastnikh.

In 2016, I finished my BASc Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo, where I spent several work terms working on a wide variety of projects, from developing tools for bioinformatics research to building online recommender systems infrastructure at Linkedin.

My current research interests include distributed systems (current flavor of the month is blockchain), distributed multi-party machine learning, attacks on multi-party ML, and corresponding defenses for these attacks. Lately, I am especially interested in privacy and security vulnerabilities in federated learning.
